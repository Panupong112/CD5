Top 5 Anime Recommendations

"Attack on Titan" - Humanity fights for survival against giant humanoid creatures.

"My Hero Academia" - A young boy dreams of becoming the greatest hero.

"Demon Slayer" - A brother's quest to save his demon-turned sister.

"Naruto" - A ninja's journey to become the strongest leader of his village.

"One Piece" - A pirate crew's epic adventure to find the ultimate treasure.
